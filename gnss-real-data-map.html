What this shows

This is real: each cell is colored by the fraction of aircraft that reported degraded GPS, using GPSJam's exact (bad−1)/(good+bad) formula. Red zones are real interference on local.

What it can't tell you yet: this feed only says GPS is degraded, not whether it's jamming (planes lose their fix) or spoofing (planes get a confident wrong fix). Both look identical here. Layer 2 (classify.py) resolves it from OpenSky position tracks: jamming shows as positions going stale while aircraft stay in contact; spoofing shows as teleporting tracks or many aircraft piled on one fake point. Run classify.py on these hot cells to label them.
